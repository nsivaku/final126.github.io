Our Mission

Our purpose is to foster discussions about how technology can be utilized to create a better world. In addition, we work to advance the national organization's mission: to empower a new generation of leaders working at the intersection of technology and social impact.

The Teams

Education Team

  • Build your foundation with training designed for beginners to upskill you to join our project teams.
  • Master industry-standard technologies including React, NextJS, Supabase, and Firebase.
  • Learn in a supportive classroom environment led by our Education Lead and Education Assistants.

Project Team

  • Work in an industry-like setting to develop tech solutions for real-world clients.
  • Build meaningful connections with teammates while supporting organizations in your own community.
  • Gain hands-on experience developing products with with real impact.
  • Experience professional development beyond the traditional classroom setting.
